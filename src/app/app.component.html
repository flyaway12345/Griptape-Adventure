Though you may not have realized it then,
You faced a fork in the road.
One path was easy.
The other unclear.

 

A key led you here.
But.
The wheels were set in motion long before that.
They started to turn at a time in your past.
A moment when you made a decision.

 

You made a choice.
And.
That choice is the true reason you are here.
It is why you are special.
It is why you received a key.

 

You notice a glimmer to the key.
You feel it hum in your hands.
“Why??”

A voice in your head says,
“This key is important.
Something tells me it must be dealt with immediately…”

To the average person,
The key you hold looks ordinary.
Something to ignore.
But.
You’re not average.
And.
It’s not ordinary.

 

You hold a Master Key.
A key that comes alive.
In the hands of someone with a unique Eye.
Someone with Vision.

 

With the key active and in your possession,
You gain the power to determine the future.
You gain access to the powerful secrets of The Waiting Place.
The opportunity to enter.
And.
The ability to choose how:
Alone.
Or.
With others.

 

“Wait.
What is The Waiting Place?”

A seductive voice in your head
whispers,
“My precious…”

You will learn more about this place soon.
But.
There is something pressing you must know first.

 

The power of the master key cannot be hoarded.
The key will not wait.
Its life is bound to something greater.
You have 72 hours to make your choice.
To exercise the full magnitude of the key’s power.

 

Should you avoid making the decision.
The key’s power may fade.
Until.
It is completely gone.

Its life may pass.
Into a key in someone else’s hands.
Someone else in your community.
Who may or may not
Invite you.

 

The key is destined to find its match.
The one with The Eye.

“Got it. But you’re going to have to tell me: What is The Waiting Place?” 

This key is an invitation to The Waiting Place.
You were one of the only people in your community to receive one.
You were hand-selected.
You are needed for what is to come.

"What is The Waiting Place?” 

You have 72 hours to make a decision.
Continue alone.
Or.
With others.

Should you avoid making a choice.
The key may pass to someone else in your community.
Someone who may or may not invite you.

“Okay. But but I’m not making any decision until you tell me:
What is The Waiting Place?” 

The key must find its match.
The one with great resolve.

“Seriously. What is The Waiting Place!?”
